Welcome to Dorem's Corner!

Hi, I'm Dorem!

Welcome to my site! This is the place where I share the things I'm passionate about.
I'm a fan of a lot of things: linguistics, history, philsophy, etc. I've always been curious about how the world works, and I enjoy diving into new ideas and seeing where they lead.

When I'm not reading or writing, I'm usually working on a project, training in martial arts, or watching anime. I also have a deep appreciation for music, whether it's rock, metal, or punk. Those are my go-to genres, but i do like to experiment and discover new styles. It changes depending on the mood I have during the day (or night).

As an Eastern Orthodox Christian, my faith is an important part of my life, though I approach it with balance and reflection. It's part of my daily routine but not something I impose on others, just a way I try to live thoughtfully.

This website is where I put all of this together: my thoughts, my work, and whatever else I'm exploring. Thanks for stopping by, and I hope something here sparks your interest!
