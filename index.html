Welcome to Dorem's Corner!

Hi, I'm Dorem, and welcome to my corner of the web!

I'm into a wide range of topics: linguistics, history, philosophy, and anything that helps me understand how the world works. I enjoy keeping myself busy, whether through reading, writing, or just thinking things through.

When I'm not buried in writing or projects (or school), you'll probably find me training in martial arts, listening to music (mostly rock, metal, or punk), or watching anime. Music shifts with my mood, and I’m always open to new sounds.

I'm also a Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) advocate. I believe in digital freedom, transparency, and the power of collaborative tools to empower individuals.

My Eastern Orthodox faith grounds me, it's a quiet constant in my life. I also have a deep interest in Orthodox mysticism and (primarily) Western esotericism, not necessarily out of belief, but out of fascination with its depth and symbolism.

This site is where I share what I’m working on and thinking about. Thanks for stopping by.
